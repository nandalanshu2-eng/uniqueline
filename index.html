Our Story

Where Heritage Defies Gravity

Antigravity Meets Indian Heritage

Uniqueline is a celebration of India's most treasured art forms — reimagined through the lens of modern aesthetics. Our collection bridges the divine world of Pichwai temple paintings with the vibrant enamel craft of Meenakari, creating pieces that seem to float between eras.

Each artwork is a testament to centuries of craftsmanship passed down through generations of master artisans from Rajasthan. We believe art should defy boundaries — transcending time, space, and gravity itself.
